Our Mission: Securing organizations in an increasingly complex digital landscape

Founded by Robert Beken, an experienced security professional with over two decades of industry experience, Cedrus Strategic is committed to providing expert cybersecurity guidance to organizations across diverse industries.

Our mission is to help organizations build resilient security programs that protect critical assets while enabling business objectives. We believe that effective security must be strategically aligned with organizational goals, practical to implement, and adaptable to evolving threats.

With experience spanning healthcare, aerospace, manufacturing, logistics, medical, finance, Wall Street, and DoD sectors, we bring a depth and breadth of knowledge that allows us to understand the unique security challenges facing different industries.

At Cedrus Strategic, we approach security holistically, considering technical controls, governance frameworks, human factors, and physical security elements. This comprehensive approach ensures that organizations develop balanced security programs that address the full spectrum of risks they face.

Our Expertise

Specialized expertise in healthcare security including HIPAA/HITECH compliance, medical device security, clinical workflow protection, and patient data privacy. We help healthcare organizations secure patient information while maintaining efficient clinical operations.
Comprehensive expertise in securing operational technology (OT) and industrial control systems (ICS) environments, including SCADA systems, manufacturing facilities, and critical infrastructure. Our approach balances security requirements with operational reliability.
